How It Works

Rinku.me streamlines the process of shortening URLs. Just follow these simple steps:

  • Paste Your URL: Copy and paste your long URL into the provided field.
  • Customize (Optional): Tailor your shortened link by specifying a custom name and expiration time. If left blank, default settings apply.
  • Generate Short Link: Click the "Shorten URL" button to generate your personalized, shortened link.

It's that easy! Your shortened link is ready to use.
